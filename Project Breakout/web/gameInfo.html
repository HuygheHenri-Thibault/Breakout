chevron_leftHow do I play?

Faction War

During the monthly Faction War, players have the chance to take up arms against the invaders or the Empire, depending on what side you pledged your allegiance to. Players will battle for fame and glory alongside their bannermen. Heroes will rise.

Field Effects

In all of your efforts along the way while completing campaigns, waging wars across the continent or playing with friends, you will be met with unique challenges called field effects. But beware, they may not necessarily be friendly...

Spells & Abilities

Making your way through the vast lands and unknown territory, you are able to collect items called scrolls. These scrolls can help you by giving you spells, and these spells can be used as abilities. However, you will have to choose what spells to use, as you only have limited power to use your magic.
